Audience Fit Score
AFS
Are you reaching the right people?

Weighted as 50% demographic match, 30% engagement match, and 20% content relevance to your campaign. Follower count is never a factor.

90–100Perfect Match
70–89Strong Match
50–69Partial Match
<50Poor Fit

AFS is weighted across three signals: demographic overlap with your target audience (50%), content engagement patterns aligned to your category (30%), and content relevance to your campaign brief (20%). Follower count is intentionally excluded — it has no predictive relationship to audience fit in controlled tests.

Brand Safety Score
BSS
How risky is this partnership?

Starts at 100. Deducted for controversy history, negative sentiment, topic risk, and volatility penalties. Know the risk before you sign.

90–100Very Safe
70–89Mostly Safe
50–69Moderate Risk
<50High Risk

BSS begins at 100 and applies deductions across four risk signals: documented controversy history, audience sentiment polarity across platforms, topic association risk (political, religious, social), and a volatility penalty for influencers with rapidly shifting public narratives. Each deduction is weighted and capped.

Performance Score
PS
Will this influencer deliver?

Weighted as 40% engagement rate, 30% growth trend, and 30% content consistency. Measures what actually drives results, not surface metrics.

90–100High-Performing
70–89Reliable
50–69Inconsistent
<50Weak

PS measures what actually predicts campaign delivery: engagement rate relative to audience size (40%), growth trajectory over the prior 90 days (30%), and content consistency — posting cadence and quality variance (30%). Surface-level metrics like raw view counts are excluded from this calculation.

Distribution Score
DS
How far does their content actually travel?

Measures content reach and distribution consistency across a creator's audience. Weighted as 40% reach volume, 40% average views, and 20% distribution consistency. Follower count is never a direct factor.

Formula DS = (0.4 × R) + (0.4 × V) + (0.2 × C)
RReach volume — normalised unique accounts reached per post relative to peer tier
VAverage views — normalised mean view count per post over trailing 90 days
CDistribution consistency — variance in reach across posts; lower variance scores higher
90–100High Distribution
70–89Good Distribution
50–69Moderate Distribution
<50Low Distribution

DS separates creators who consistently distribute content across their audience from those with inflated follower counts but narrow reach. R and V are each normalised against a creator's peer tier so a micro-creator with excellent reach scores comparably to a macro-creator with equivalent efficiency. C penalises erratic distribution — one viral post masking consistently low reach is caught here.

CPI
Creator Performance Index
Scores the creator. Combines audience fit, performance, distribution, and brand safety into one number.
Formula CPI = 0.28×AFS + 0.22×PS + 0.25×DS + 0.25×BSS
80–100Strong Go
65–79Conditional Go
50–64Borderline
<50No Go
BQS
Brand / Offer Quality Score
Scores the offer. Combines offer strength and communicability to ensure the product is campaign-ready.
Formula BQS = 0.60×O + 0.40×S  ·  Final = 0.50×CPI + 0.50×BQS
OOffer Strength
SSimplicity
